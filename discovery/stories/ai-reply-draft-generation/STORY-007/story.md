# Story: STORY-007 — Generate AI reply draft for a selected conversation

**Capability:** AI reply draft generation
**Feature:** AI Draft Generation
**Business Goal:** BG-001
**Priority:** Must-have
**Hypothesis:** [./hypothesis.md](./hypothesis.md)

---

## Job Story

**When** I have identified a relevant conversation that I want to engage with
**I want to** generate an AI reply draft that matches my voice and adds genuine value
**So I can** save significant time on drafting while maintaining reply quality and authenticity

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a selected conversation with context and participant information, when I request an AI draft, then a reply draft is generated within 30 seconds that references key points from the conversation

2. Given a conversation and my historical content as style reference, when the AI generates a draft, then the editing time required is less than 2.5 minutes on average based on beta user feedback

3. Given a generated draft, when I review it, then I can easily identify which parts reference the original conversation context and which parts add my perspective

4. Given multiple draft generation requests, when I use the same conversation, then the system provides variance in approach while maintaining consistent voice quality

5. Given a draft that doesn't meet my expectations, when I regenerate it, then alternative angles or tones are offered that better match my intent
