# Story: STORY-008 — Regenerate draft with alternative tone or angle

**Capability:** AI reply draft generation
**Feature:** AI Draft Generation
**Business Goal:** BG-001
**Priority:** Must-have
**Hypothesis:** [./hypothesis.md](./hypothesis.md)

---

## Job Story

**When** I'm not satisfied with the initial AI-generated draft and want to explore different approaches
**I want to** regenerate the draft with alternative tones or angles that better match my intent
**So I can** find the perfect reply approach faster and increase my satisfaction with the draft generation feature

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given an existing AI-generated draft, when I request regeneration, then I am presented with at least 3 alternative options with different tones (professional, casual, humorous) or angles (question-based, value-focused, storytelling)

2. Given multiple regeneration requests for the same conversation, when I compare options, then each alternative provides genuinely different approaches rather than minor variations

3. Given a regenerated draft, when I evaluate it against the original, then I can clearly identify the differences in tone or angle applied

4. Given successful regeneration usage, when I track my workflow, then my average draft approval time decreases by at least 20% compared to manual editing

5. Given the regeneration feature, when I use it regularly, then at least 60% of my draft interactions involve trying alternative options before final approval
