# Story: STORY-023 — Score conversation authors on relevance to builder's niche

**Capability:** Author fit / audience relevance scoring
**Feature:** Author Fit Scoring
**Business Goal:** BG-002
**Priority:** Must-have
**Hypothesis:** [./hypothesis.md](./hypothesis.md)

---

## Job Story

**When** I'm evaluating conversation opportunities and want to maximize the likelihood of meaningful engagement
**I want to** see author fit scores that indicate how relevant conversation participants are to my niche and likely to engage back
**So I can** focus my engagement efforts on conversations where the original poster and participants are more likely to respond and engage meaningfully

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a conversation with identifiable authors, when the system analyzes their profiles, then I see an author fit score based on profile keywords, recent content topics, and audience overlap with my niche

2. Given conversations with high author fit scores, when I engage with them, then at least 40% of my replies receive a response within 24 hours, compared to 15% for random conversations

3. Given the author fit scoring algorithm, when I compare different conversations, then I can clearly see which authors contribute most to the fit score and why

4. Given a conversation with mixed author fit (some relevant, some not), when the system calculates the overall score, then the explanation for the weighting is transparent and actionable

5. Given the author fit feature, when I use it regularly, then my time spent on conversations with low-response-probability authors decreases by at least 30% compared to manual evaluation
