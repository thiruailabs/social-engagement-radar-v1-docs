# Story: STORY-027 — Aggregate outcome data to identify high-performing reply patterns

**Capability:** Reply performance feedback loop
**Feature:** Outcome-Driven Score Refinement
**Business Goal:** BG-002
**Priority:** Should-have
**Hypothesis:** [./hypothesis.md](./hypothesis.md)

---

## Job Story

**When** I want to improve my engagement strategy based on what has worked in the past
**I want to** see aggregated outcome data that identifies high-performing reply patterns and content characteristics from my successful engagements
**So I can** refine my approach and focus on conversations where my reply style historically generated positive outcomes

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a sufficient volume of outcome data (minimum 50 recorded outcomes), when the system analyzes successful replies, then at least 3 common high-performing patterns are identified (e.g., question-based opens, value statements, personal references)

2. Given identified high-performing reply patterns, when applied to new conversations, then these conversations show a correlation coefficient of at least 0.4 between pattern usage and positive outcomes

3. Given the pattern analysis feature, when I review my engagement history, then I can clearly see which reply styles and content approaches led to the best outcomes

4. Given conversations that match high-performing patterns, when the opportunity scoring algorithm incorporates this data, then their scores are at least 25% higher than average conversations

5. Given the feedback loop in action, when I use the refined scoring in Phase 3 testing, then I report at least 20% more measurable outcomes compared to baseline performance
