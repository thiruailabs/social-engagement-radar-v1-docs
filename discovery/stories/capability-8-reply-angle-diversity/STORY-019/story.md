# Story: STORY-019 — Generate multiple reply angles for a single conversation

**Capability:** Reply angle diversity
**Feature:** Multi-Angle Draft Generation
**Business Goal:** BG-002
**Priority:** Must-have
**Hypothesis:** [./hypothesis.md](./hypothesis.md)

---

## Job Story

**When** I'm preparing to engage with a conversation and want to maximize my chances of meaningful participation
**I want to** generate multiple diverse reply angles (question-based, value-focused, storytelling, contrarian) for each opportunity
**So I can** find the most effective approach for each situation and increase overall conversation participation rates

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a conversation opportunity, when I request reply angles, then I receive at least 3-4 distinct approaches with clearly different tones or perspectives (question-based, value-focused, storytelling, contrarian)

2. Given multiple reply angles for the same conversation, when I compare them, then each angle provides genuinely different value propositions rather than superficial wording changes

3. Given conversations with multiple reply options, when users select and send different angles, then at least 2 angle types show response rates that are 25% higher than others in beta testing

4. Given the multi-angle generation feature, when I use it regularly, then my overall conversation engagement rate increases by at least 20% compared to single-draft approaches

5. Given successful angle generation, when I track my participation metrics, then I can identify which angle types work best for different conversation contexts and adjust my strategy accordingly
